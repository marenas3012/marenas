About Me

Get to know me better!

Hello!

I am an international student from Lima, Peru studying at BYU, majoring in Pre-Business and aspiring to be part of the Global Supply Chain Management Program.

Most Important Experiences:

Founder of Kind Totes: Kind Totes is a small business where I sell eco-friendly tote bags with hand-embroidery designs made by myself through social media. I have been able to develop my embroidery and social media sales skills, as well as learn how to run a business.

Marketing Intern at Ecolimpio: For the last two summers I worked as a marketing intern at Ecolimpio Peru, a company that provides green cleaning services. During this job, I managed the company’s social media networks and created content for them such as Facebook and Instagram posts and stories. I learned about social media management in a corporate environment, and I was able to apply what I learned to my own entrepreneurship.

Delegate at Model United Nations: For the last five years, I’ve been actively attending MUN conferences, where I have been able to represent different countries in several United Nations committees. This experience led me to improve my public speaking, leadership, diplomacy, and negotiation skills.

Volunteer at Hogar Pendiente: was part of a group of volunteers with the Hogar Pendiente campaign, in which we collected a large amount of money virtually, asking for donations through social media for a shelter that provides accommodations for poor families who go to Lima for health treatments through donations.

Awards and Recognitions:

2021 - FEPUC Sustainable Entrepreneurship Fair Guest Speaker: The recognition was for explaining the beginning of my small business, as well as my value proposition and the features of the products I offer at the FEPUC’s Sustainable Entrepreneurship Fair.

2022 - University of West London Certificate of Distinction in Piano Performance: I obtained a distinction award for my performance on the London College of Music Examinations Digital Exam, achieving 100 out of 100 points.

Languages:

I speak Spanish as my native language, bilingual in English, and elementary proficiency in German and Portuguese.
